Early Life

M.S. Dhoni (born July 7, 1981, Ranchi, Bihar [now Jharkhand] state, India) is an Indian cricketer whose rise to prominence in the early 21st century culminated in his captaincy of the Indian national team that won the one-day Cricket World Cup in 2011. Dhoni made his international debut in 2004.11 Dec 2024. From a young age, he displayed exceptional talent and passion for cricket, eventually making his way to the national team.

Achievements

Dhoni is celebrated for his incredible leadership as the captain of the Indian cricket team, leading India to victory in the 2007 T20 World Cup, the 2011 ODI World Cup, and the 2013 Champions Trophy He is also known for his exceptional finishing skills and calm demeanor under pressure.Mahendra Singh Dhoni, often called 'Captain Cool,' is a cricket icon and former Indian cricket team captain. Renowned for his calm demeanor and strategic prowess, Dhoni led India to numerous victories, including the 2007 ICC T20 World Cup and the 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup.
